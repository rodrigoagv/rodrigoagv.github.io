About me

I was born in September 24th, 1992 in Viña del Mar, Chile. In November 2016 I received the MSc. in Electronic Engineering degree with a major in Automatic Control, together with my Electronics Engineering degree, at Universidad Técnica Federico Santa María (UTFSM) in Valparaíso, Chile.

My MSc. Thesis was on "Enforcement of causality and passivity in spectral analysis", and was supervised by Dr. Ricardo Rojas from UTFSM along with Dr. Cristian Rojas and Dr. Patricio Valenzuela from KTH Royal Institute of Technology in Stockholm, Sweden (KTH).

On October 2017, I began my Ph.D. studies at KTH under the supervision of Cristian R. Rojas . My main interests are among the areas of system identification, signal processing, and control theory.

Among other interests, I enjoy running, playing soccer and chess. I also like playing guitar (acoustic and electric), bass (fretted and fretless), and keyboards. I speak English fluently and Spanish is my native language.
