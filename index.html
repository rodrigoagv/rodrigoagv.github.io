About me

I was born on September 24, 1992, in Viña del Mar, Chile. In November 2016, I received an M.Sc. degree in Electronic Engineering with a major in Automatic Control, together with my Electronics Engineering degree, from Universidad Técnica Federico Santa María (UTFSM) in Valparaíso, Chile, where I was awarded the Best Electronic Engineering Student Award. I received my Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering from KTH Royal Institute of Technology in Stockholm, Sweden, in May 2022. Since June 2022, I have been affiliated with the Control Systems Technology Section at Eindhoven University of Technology, the Netherlands, where I currently serve as an Assistant Professor.

My research focuses on the design and statistical analysis of data-driven modelling, estimation, and control methods, with applications in high-tech systems.
