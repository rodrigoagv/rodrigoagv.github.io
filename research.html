Research and Publications

In my MSc. Thesis, I worked on Non-Parametric System Identification. I currently do research on Continuous-time System Identification and finite-sample analysis of System Identification methods. I have also obtained results in Non-parametric System Identification, Control over Networks, and Riccati Equations.
