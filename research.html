Research

My research is centered around the design and statistical analysis of algorithms for system identification, state estimation, fault detection and control, with applications in the high-tech precision industry.

If you are interested in the latest pdf version of any of the manuscripts that are mentioned here, please send me an e-mail. I am happy to discuss any topic!
