Research and Publications

In my MSc Thesis, I worked on Non-Parametric System Identification. I currently do research on Continuous-time
System Identification and finite-sample analysis of System Identification methods. I have also produced results in
Impulse Response Estimation, Control over Networks, and Riccati Equations.
