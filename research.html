About me

I was born in September 24th, 1992 in Viña del Mar, Chile. In November 2016 I received the MSc in Electronic Engineering degree with a major in Automatic Control, together with my Electronics Engineering degree, in Universidad Técnica Federico Santa María (UTFSM) in Valparaíso, Chile.

My MSc Thesis (written in spanish) is named "Enforcement of causality and passivity in spectral analysis", and was supervised by Ricardo Rojas, together with Cristian Rojas and Patricio Valenzuela from KTH.
